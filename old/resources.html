Aviation Safety FAQ

Air travel is extremelly safe, but it's normal, as a passenger, expecially if you suffer of anxiety when you fly, to have a lot of questions regarding safety. Sometimes yout questions are driven by your anxiety, sometimes by a recent accident which makes you doubt about the safety of your next flight.

Don't worry, this section contains the most frequently asked questions by nervous fliers and a precise and comprehensive anser for each of them. And if the question you have in mind is not listed here, you can always contact us and we will do our best to point you to other resources that will solve your doubts.
